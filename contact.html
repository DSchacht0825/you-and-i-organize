Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our organizing and cleaning services

How long does a typical organizing project take?

Project timelines vary based on the size and complexity of your space. Most single rooms take 4-8 hours, while whole home projects can take several days. We'll provide a timeline estimate during your free consultation.

Do I need to be present during the organizing session?

We prefer that you're present, especially for decluttering decisions. However, we can work independently for certain tasks like cleaning and arranging once systems are established and preferences are clear.

Do you provide organizing supplies and containers?

We can provide organizing supplies and containers as part of our service, or we can work with containers you already have. We'll discuss the best approach during your consultation.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve San Diego, La Jolla, Del Mar, Encinitas, and surrounding areas. Contact us to confirm if we service your specific location.

How much do your services cost?

Our rates vary based on the type and scope of service. We offer competitive hourly rates and package deals. Contact us for a free estimate tailored to your specific needs.

Do you offer maintenance services?

Yes! We offer ongoing maintenance services to help you keep your spaces organized. This can include monthly, quarterly, or seasonal touch-ups and re-organization as needed.
