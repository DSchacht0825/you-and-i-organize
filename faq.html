Common Questions About Home Organizing Services

Everything you need to know about working with you&I organize

How much does professional home organizing cost in San Diego?

Professional home organizing services in San Diego typically range from $150-$400 per session, depending on the scope of work. Whole home organizing projects range from $150-$300 per session, while specialty services like moving organization can cost $300-$800. We offer free consultations to provide accurate pricing based on your specific needs.

What areas does you&I organize serve?

you&I organize provides professional home organizing and cleaning services throughout San Diego County, including San Diego, La Jolla, Del Mar, Encinitas, Carlsbad, Solana Beach, Rancho Santa Fe, and surrounding areas. We travel up to 30 miles from central San Diego.

How long does a typical home organizing session take?

Most organizing sessions last 3-6 hours, depending on the size of the space and scope of work. A single room like a bedroom or office typically takes 3-4 hours, while larger spaces like kitchens or whole home projects may require multiple sessions. We work at your pace and can break larger projects into manageable sessions.

Do I need to buy organizing supplies and storage containers?

We recommend waiting until after your initial organizing session to purchase supplies. During the organizing process, we'll identify exactly what storage solutions you need and can recommend specific products. This prevents over-buying and ensures you get the right containers for your space and needs.

What should I do to prepare for an organizing session?

Minimal preparation is needed! We recommend clearing any personal items you want to keep private and having cleaning supplies available. Don't pre-sort or organize - that's what we're here for! Just ensure we have access to the spaces we'll be working on and any storage areas.

Do you help with decluttering and deciding what to keep?

Yes! Decluttering is a core part of our organizing process. We help you sort through items using proven decision-making frameworks, but all final decisions about what to keep, donate, or discard are yours. We provide guidance and support throughout the process with a compassionate, non-judgmental approach.

How do I maintain the organized systems after you're done?

We teach you simple maintenance strategies during each session and provide written guidelines for keeping your spaces organized. Most clients find the systems easy to maintain because they're designed specifically for your lifestyle. We also offer follow-up sessions and maintenance services if needed.

Do you offer cleaning services along with organizing?

Yes! We offer comprehensive organize & clean packages that combine professional organizing with deep cleaning services. This is perfect for getting a fresh start in your newly organized spaces. We also provide standalone deep cleaning services and recurring maintenance cleaning.

Can you help with moving and packing?

Absolutely! Our moving & transition services include pre-move decluttering, professional packing, unpacking in your new home, and setting up organized systems from day one. This service saves time and ensures you start organized in your new space rather than living out of boxes.

What makes you&I organize different from other organizing services?

you&I organize offers a uniquely compassionate approach led by certified professional organizer Marissa Morales. As a mother of four, Marissa understands family life challenges. We focus on sustainable systems tailored to your specific lifestyle, offer combined organizing and cleaning services, and maintain the highest client satisfaction ratings in San Diego.
